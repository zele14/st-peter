About Us

Saint Peter and Paul Family Choir was established on the first Sunday of Advent 2018 at the Cathedral Parish Saint Michel. It aims to unite university students and graduates through music.

Historical Background

Saints Peter and Paul Family Choir began with an idea from MUHIRE Paul, aiming to form a mass choir of university students. The choir includes members from various university choirs in Kigali.

Reasons for Formation

The choir was formed to promote the talents of university choir members, support Catholic choirs, and unite various choirs to achieve its goals.

Establishment

The choir was officially established in a meeting held on 22nd September 2018, with the first mass celebrated on 2nd December 2018. The name was later changed to Saint Peter and Paul Family Choir.
